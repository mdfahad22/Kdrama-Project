Everyone knows that most hospitals are boring, but this isn't the case. This hospital is calming but exciting. Why is this hospital exciting, you may ask. Well, it's because of 5 doctors who make the boring hospital halls into colorful mall-like halls! They've been friends ever since they were in med school, but thanks to their occupations as doctors, they're closer than ever! The friends decide to create a band, but this is a bit of a problem because the lead singer has little experience when it comes to singing. When she sings, she sounds like a dying goat! The King: Eternal-Monarch



The King: Eternal Monarch was one of 2022s most anticipated Korean drama which aired between the 17th April-12th June. It features actors such as Lee Minho, who is well known for his role in The Heirs and Kim Go Eun from Goblin. It also features screenwriter Kim Eun Sook who worked on Goblin and Director Baek Sang-Hoon who worked on Decedents of The Sun. So The Eternal Monarch has a lot of top industry names, but sadly it failed to hit all the marks for me.
